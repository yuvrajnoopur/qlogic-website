Common Questions

My child is weak in maths. Is this camp right for them?

Absolutely. This camp is designed to build confidence and understanding from the basics. Vedic Maths makes calculations intuitive — even students who struggle with traditional methods respond well.

Maximum 15 students per batch. This is intentional — small groups mean personal attention, and every child gets to participate actively.

At Q-Logic Labs institute, opposite LIDG DAV School on Railway Road, Jalalabad. It's a safe, dedicated classroom space.

Can I sit in and watch a class?

Yes. Parents are welcome to observe the first class to see the teaching methodology firsthand.

You can pay at the institute (cash or UPI) or via a payment link we'll share on WhatsApp after registration. Register first — payment can be completed before June 5.
