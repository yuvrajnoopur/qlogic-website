This Privacy Policy explains how Q-Logic Labs ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website qlogic.co.in or enrol in our programs. We are committed to handling your data responsibly and in line with applicable Indian law, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as the parent's and student's name, phone number, email, school/class, exam interest, and details submitted via our forms or WhatsApp. When you make a payment, the transaction is processed by our payment partner; we receive confirmation details (such as a payment ID and amount) but do not collect or store your card, UPI, or bank credentials.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process enrolments and payments, schedule and deliver classes, send confirmations and receipts, respond to enquiries, share class-related updates, and improve our services. We may contact you by phone, WhatsApp, SMS, or email about your enrolment and relevant programs.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share it only with service providers who help us operate — primarily our payment processor, Razorpay (which maintains its own PCI-DSS-compliant security) — and where required by law.

4. Data Security & Retention

We take reasonable measures to protect your information against unauthorised access or misuse. We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics to understand site usage and improve performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Many of our students are minors. We collect a child's information only through a parent or guardian for the purpose of providing our educational services.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and withdraw consent for non-essential communications, by contacting us at qlogic.jbd@gmail.com.
